Our Ecosystem Model in Practice

Advanced Clinical Practice

Donnie feels his heart racing and dizzy. He arrives to the clinic in atrial fibrillation. A low value doctor would send him to the ER immediately.

Dr. Rita is a Hue Health Advanced Provider. She has dealt with this in the hospital. She realizes that patient is stable. She establishes an IV line for medications.

Dr. Rita gives an IV medication that slows the heart rate. She controls his rate and observes him before transitioning to oral medication.

Dr. Rita avoids an unnecessary hospitalization. She meets with Donnie in the office the following day where he tolerated his treatment well.

Outpatient Surgery

Suzie has Uterine Fibroids which causes her to miss work & end up in the ER for pain medications up to 4 times a year.

Suzie sees her Hue Health Advanced Provider and together they decide that surgery is best course. Hue Health Care Coordinators call to coordinate a time for Suzie's surgery that maximizes quality and value.

The surgeon operates on Mondays at a hospital that charges $20K but on Wednesday she operates at a surgical center that only costs $4K.

A Hue Health nurse practictioner follows up with her a few days after her surgery to check in and make sure she feeling ok and has no complications.

Suzie sees the same surgeon and has the same surgery, but saves 5x on the procedure. She is happy,and most importantly pain free.
