How it Works

Step 1: Optimizing Clinic Locations

We start by identifying locations to deploy our modular clinics. Want them on-site, near-site, in-office, or near where your employees live? No problem! Your employees can also use our existing network of clinics.

Our first clinic can be set up in as little as 3 weeks. In the meantime, your employees are free to use our 24/7 virtual services whenever they like.

For 90-95% of your employees, Hue Health is everything care. We cover all primary care, urgent care, chronic care management, x-rays, labs, and more, for no additional charges or copays. Our model allows us to extend our services beyond what normal primary care can provide.

Step 4: Reducing Waste

Hospitals can be expensive. We do everything we possibly can to keep our patients healthy to avoid unnecessary hospital visits. But if they have to, we know the right hospitals to save you even more.

Step 5: Performance-Based Pricing

A small flat fee gives your employees access to the entire Hue Health network. But to make sure our care ecosystem is providing high quality care and reducing your claims cost, the majority of our revenue model is put on performance-based pricing. Only pay full price if we deliver on what we say we can.
