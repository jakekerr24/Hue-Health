A Truly Transformative Healthcare Experience

We believe in giving back to everyone who has ever overpaid for healthcare, had a horrible experience, or hasn't had access to quality care. This is the way healthcare should be.

We're building something better because we've seen the problems firsthand

"In 2016, I suffered my first ischemic attack.

I experienced the inefficiencies of the healthcare system firsthand as I was passed around from specialist to specialist with no real answers. So when I teamed up with Lewis to create Hue Health, I wanted to create a system of care that supports everyone - patients and providers - and provides the highest quality of care possible while negating inefficiencies. No more waiting for appointments, no more unanswered questions, no more complicated insurance bills or administrative burdens, and no more anxiety about going to the doctor. Hue Health is truly a transformative experience because we’ve seen all of the problems first hand.”


Jake Kerr, Co-Founder of Hue Health
